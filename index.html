Chattel Mortgage: A Smart Financing Option for Business Vehicles

What is a Chattel Mortgage?

A Chattel Mortgage is a business finance product where a lender provides funds to purchase a vehicle or equipment, and the borrower takes ownership while the lender holds a mortgage over the asset until the loan is repaid.

Key Benefits of a Chattel Mortgage

  • Tax Benefits: Claim GST, depreciation, and interest expenses if used for business purposes.
  • Immediate Ownership: You own the asset from the start while repaying the loan.
  • Flexible Loan Terms: Choose repayment options that suit your business cash flow.
  • Balloon Payment Options: Reduce monthly repayments with a final lump sum payment.
  • Competitive Interest Rates: Secured loan structure ensures lower rates.

How Does a Chattel Mortgage Work?

The process is straightforward and beneficial for businesses:

  1. Select the vehicle or equipment your business needs.
  2. Apply for a Chattel Mortgage with flexible repayment options.
  3. Take ownership of the asset while the lender holds a mortgage.
  4. Claim tax benefits and enjoy structured repayments.
  5. Once fully repaid, the lender releases the mortgage.
